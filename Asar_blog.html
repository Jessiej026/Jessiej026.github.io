Last summer was the first summer I partcipated in the orientation leadeer program. Serving as a guide and mentor to incoming students was not just a responsibility but a privilege. Each interaction, whether it was helping someone find their way on campus or offering words of encouragement, left a lasting impression on me. As an orientation leader, I not only welcomed new faces but also forged connections that I'll carry with me long after the summer ended.
