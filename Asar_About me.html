    SO WHO AM I?

    My name is Jessica Asare. I am a rising junior studying Cybersecuirty and Business at Mount St. Marys University. I live in Maryland but am from Ghana.I spend my free time exploring the area, finding cool spots with my friends and marking them.

    Fun Facts...

    I've got a love for all things living, from Elephants to medicinal herbs. My favorite color is pink and pastel colors. I'm a foodie at heart and enjoy trying dishes from different parts of the world. Some of my favorite things to do are watching the sunrise and sunset, taking pictures of beautiful scenery, spending time alone, spending time with friends and family, and learning more about myself and the world. I'm a firm believer in the power of positivity and mindfulness.Embracing my motto of taking things one day at a time, I find peace and joy in each moment, knowing that everything unfolds as it's meant to.
